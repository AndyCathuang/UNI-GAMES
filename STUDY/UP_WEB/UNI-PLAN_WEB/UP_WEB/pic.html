游戏简介(CN)

•UNI-PLAN是一款由UNI-GAMES®制作的一款星际冒险游戏。

•游戏的背景设定在一个广袤无垠的宇宙中，玩家将化身为一名勇敢的冒险者，探索那些未知的星际领域。

•这款游戏不仅采用了先进的虚幻引擎技术，带来了惊艳的视觉效果和沉浸式的游戏体验，还结合了独特的游戏机制，让玩家在每一次冒险中都能感受到新鲜和刺激。

•在游戏中，玩家需要面对各种各样的挑战，包括神秘的外星生物、复杂的星际任务以及未知的宇宙现象。通过不断探索和解锁新的技能，玩家能够提升自己的能力，并与其他玩家合作，完成更为艰巨的任务。

•UNI-PLAN不仅提供了丰富的游戏内容，还鼓励玩家之间的互动与交流，提升了游戏的乐趣与深度。

•如果你喜欢不可预测的冒险旅程，就来UNI-PLAN吧！

Game introduction(EN)

UNI-PLAN is an intergalactic adventure game produced by UNI-GAMES®.

•The game is set in a vast expanse of the universe, the player will be a brave adventurer, explore those unknown interstellar realms.

•The game not only uses advanced Unreal Engine technology to bring stunning visuals and immersive gaming experience, but also combines unique game mechanics to make players feel fresh and exciting in every adventure.

•In the game, players face a wide variety of challenges, including mysterious alien creatures, complex interstellar missions, and unknown cosmic phenomena. By constantly exploring and unlocking new skills, players can improve their abilities and cooperate with other players to complete more difficult tasks.

•UNI-PLAN not only provides rich game content, but also encourages interaction and communication between players, enhancing the fun and depth of the game.

•If you like an unpredictable adventure, come to UNI-PLAN!
