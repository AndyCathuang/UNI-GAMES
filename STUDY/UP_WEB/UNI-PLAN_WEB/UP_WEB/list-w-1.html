UNI-PLAN主线剧情玩法介绍(CN)

主线剧情模式的背景设定在2400年。

随着科技的发展太阳系资源急速枯竭，人类社会的发展也面临着前所未有的挑战。

在这样的条件下，人类必须向其他的星系进行移民。

在这个模式里你将扮演《远航》计划的指挥官，带领《远航》计划的飞船及成员们，前往其他星系，寻找适合居住的环境，并在那里建立起新的家园。

UNI-PLAN Main Story Gameplay Introduction (EN)

Main story mode is set in the year 2400.

With the development of science and technology, the resources of the solar system are rapidly depleted, and the development of human society is also facing unprecedented challenges.

Under such conditions, humans would have to migrate to other star systems.

In this mode, you will play as the commander of the "Voyage" program, leading the "Voyage" program ships and members to other galaxies, searching for habitable environments, and establishing new homes there.
